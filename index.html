Regardless of your age, occupation, gender, individual ability or aspirations, an experienced podiatrist can improve your mobility, independence and contribute to a better quality of life.

North Devon’s longest established foot and ankle experts

Our team of Podiatrists are members of the Royal College of Podiatry which is regulated by the Health Care Professions Council.

From problematic skin and nail conditions to complex gait, biomechanical and soft tissue issues, North Devon's experienced and progressive foot and ankle specialists are able to formulate a treatment plan to suit your individual care.

Sam, with 30 years experience as a podiatrist, is an independent healthcare professional, her aim is to achieve palliative, preventative and curative foot care to people of all backgrounds.
